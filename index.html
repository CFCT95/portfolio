About Me

Hi 👋 I’m Cristian — a business strategist turned tech enthusiast, with more than 10 years of experience in logistics, international trade, and sourcing. I’ve helped companies grow globally by building smart supply chains, negotiating with international partners, and leading projects that actually move the needle.

But here’s the twist: I don’t just stay on the “business” side. 🚀 I bring technology into the mix — using automation and AI to solve complex challenges and create scalable solutions. My toolkit includes Make, n8n, Excel, Google Sheets, ChatGPT, Gemini, DeepSeek, Gamma, and whatever new tool sparks innovation.

I believe the future of business is all about the sweet spot between strategy and technology. That’s why I focus on projects where I can blend negotiation, logistics know-how, and cutting-edge tools to make workflows smarter, faster, and more human.

Some of my career highlights include roles as Sourcing Specialist at Sonicare Solutions Inc., Sales Account Executive at Laufer Group International, Account Executive at BSG Logistics, and Procurement & Logistics Coordinator at Vermeer VE Group. Each role pushed me to think differently, innovate, and balance business growth with tech-driven impact.
